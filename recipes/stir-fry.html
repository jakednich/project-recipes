Stir Fry

Description:

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Chicken
  • Onions
  • Ginger and garlic
  • Broccoli
  • Sauce
  • Brown sugar
  • Seasonings

How to make:

  1. Coat chickne in cornstard and cook until crispy
  2. Add onions, ginger, and garlic
  3. Cook broccoli in the skillet
  4. Make sauce
  5. Add chicken mixture to the broccoli, then soy sauce mixture
  6. Cook until the chicken and broccoli are well-coated with thickened sauce
