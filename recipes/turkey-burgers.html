Turkey Burgers

Description:

Ingredients

  • Ground turkey
  • Bread crumbs
  • Diced onions
  • Egg whites
  • Parsely
  • Garlic
  • Seasonings

How to make:

  1. Mix all ingredients into a bowl
  2. Form patties
  3. Cook patties on skillet over medium heat
  4. Assemble burger with fixings of your choice
  5. Serve and enjoy!
